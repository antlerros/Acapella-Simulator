Users could use Acapella Simulator to record audio files and adjust the speed, 
pitch and volume of them and do the mixing to make music accompanied by their own voice.

=====================
=== How to Use It ===
=====================

Run OneManAcapella.m on Matlab.
After the GUI pop out, users could make their own music
